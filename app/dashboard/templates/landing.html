Why ClearSight

Eye care that fits your life

From first symptom to booked appointment — fast, accurate, and compassionate.

Voice & Text Triage

Speak or type your symptoms. Our AI listens, asks the right questions, and assesses urgency in real time.

Eye Image Analysis

Take a photo of your eye. Our AI visually observes redness, discharge, and other visible features to support assessment.

Instant Booking

Once triage is complete, your appointment is booked automatically based on urgency — no waiting on hold.

Remembers Your History

ClearSight remembers your past visits and symptoms, making every triage faster and more personalised.

Private & Secure

Confirmation by Email

Get your appointment details, urgency level, and care instructions sent directly to your email after every session.
